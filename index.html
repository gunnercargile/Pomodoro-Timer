Why Use the Pomodoro Technique?

Science backs the Pomodoro Technique's effectiveness! Our brains focus best in short bursts.

By working in 25-minute intervals with planned breaks, you combat mental fatigue, avoid procrastination, and boost productivity.

Short deadlines keep you motivated, while tracking your Pomodoros helps you understand your work style and manage time effectively.
